Preparation Method for GUM SYRUP
  • Get all the ingredients ready
  • Add the juice to the blender on low speed
  • Add Acacia Gum and Blend on low speed
  • Add sugar and Blend on Low
  • Weigh the final volume of syrup and add the citric acid
  • Bottle and keep in refrigerator for 2 weeks
Preparation Method for Regular Syrup
  • Heat your liquid and the additional sugar over gentle heat on a stovetop or in a microwave, and stir just until the sugar is dissolved and everything is combined
  • Allow the mixture to cool at room temperature, then portion into squeeze bottles, and clearly label and date all bottles
  • Store 1:1 syrups in the refrigerator for up to 1 month and 2:1 syrups for up to 6 weeks
  • Check for signs of spoilage before use. When in doubt, always discard and make a new batch
